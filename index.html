Store Policy

We stand behind every item we make. Please read our return policy before placing your order.

Return Window

Returns are accepted within 15 days of the date you received your order. Items returned after 15 days will not be accepted.

Restocking Fee

All accepted returns are subject to a 15% restocking fee deducted from your refund. This applies to all items unless the return is due to our error.

Return Shipping

The buyer is responsible for all return shipping costs. We recommend using a tracked shipping method. Fun Travel is not responsible for items lost in return transit.

Get an RMA First

Do not ship anything back without contacting us first. Email picswelove2024@gmail.com to receive a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number before sending your return.

Our Error — Full Refund

If we sent the wrong item or your order arrived damaged due to our fault, contact us immediately. We will cover return shipping and issue a full refund or replacement — no restocking fee.

Condition of Returns

Items must be returned in original, unworn, and undamaged condition with all original packaging. Used or altered items will not be accepted for return.
